Grabio Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Grabio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Grabio mobile application (Android and iOS) and the website at grabio.space. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in with Google.
  • Order information: Items ordered, delivery details, and order history.
  • Device information: Device identifiers and push notification tokens.
  • Photos and camera: Used only when you choose to upload a product or profile photo. We do not access your camera in the background.
  • Location data: For authorized sales representatives using Sales CRM features, we collect approximate location only when they log a field visit and grant location permission. Location is not collected continuously or in the background.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process and fulfill orders
  • To send push notifications about orders and account activity
  • To support Sales CRM workflows for authorized store representatives
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve our services

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only as needed to operate the service:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, and push notifications
  • Store owners: Order and customer information required to fulfill orders
  • Service providers: Trusted infrastructure providers that process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards

4. Location Data

Location is optional and used only for Sales CRM visit logging by authorized representatives. Representatives can deny location permission and still use other app features. We do not use location for advertising or unrelated tracking.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls such as Firebase security rules, to protect your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Grabio is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect data regardless of processing location.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at support@grabio.space.
