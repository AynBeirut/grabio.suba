How to pitch in 3 steps

1
Open with the pain — don't mention Grabio yet
"How are you managing your orders and inventory right now? WhatsApp and Excel? And how much time does that take you every day?"
Let them talk. Every "we use WhatsApp" is your opening. Every "we lose track of orders" is your close.
2
Show — don't tell
"Let me show you something quickly — a manufacturer I work with was doing exactly that. Now they have a live store, their inventory updates automatically on every sale, and they send PDF invoices in one click. This is Grabio."
Pull out your phone. Show the store. Show the dashboard. Show an invoice being generated. 60 seconds is enough.
3
Close with zero risk
"You can start completely free — no credit card. Try it for 3 months, see if it works for your business. If you like it, it's $10 a month. Less than one delivery fee."
Send them to grabio.space right there. Help them register on the spot if they're willing. First impression matters.
