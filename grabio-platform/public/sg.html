01 — What is Grabio

One platform. Every tool a small business needs.

Grabio is a cloud-based commerce and inventory platform built for small Lebanese and MENA businesses that want to sell online, manage their stock, and run their operations — all from a single dashboard or mobile app. From the moment a customer discovers your store on grabio.space, to the second an order ships and payment lands, Grabio handles it: online storefront, real-time inventory, purchase orders, staff payroll, invoicing, analytics, and multi-currency checkout (USD/LBP, OMT & Stripe). No expensive developers, no complicated setup — just sign up and your store goes live in minutes.

05 — How to Pitch It

3-step sales script

Step 1 — Open with a problem

Find the pain point

"Hey [name], quick question — right now, how do you keep track of your stock and know what's selling? Are you using Excel? WhatsApp? Your memory?"

Listen. Let them answer. Most will mention a messy spreadsheet or nothing at all. That's your opening.

Step 2 — Introduce the solution

Show them Grabio on your phone

"There's an app called Grabio, made specifically for businesses here in Lebanon. It gives you a full online store, tracks your inventory in real time, handles invoices, and even lets your customers pay with OMT. You can start completely free — you only pay 20% of what you sell, nothing upfront. After that, it's just $10 a month to keep 100% of your sales."

Show the app live. Pull up grabio.space on your phone. Nothing sells better than seeing an actual store demo.

Step 3 — Close with a low-risk ask

Remove the barrier to yes

"The best part? It costs you nothing to try. I can help you set up your store right now in about 15 minutes. What do you think — want to get started?"

Don't ask if they're interested. Ask if they want to start right now. Offer to set it up with them on the spot — that converts.
