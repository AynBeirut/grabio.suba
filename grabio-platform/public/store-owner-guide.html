Inventory & Stock Control

Know what you have. Always.

Full warehouse-grade inventory management — from raw materials to finished goods — in a system any shop owner can use.

🧪

Raw Materials

Track ingredients and components with minimum thresholds, reorder points, expiry dates, and warranty periods.

📋

Recipes & Production

Define recipes with ingredient lists, production yield, and cost per unit. Trigger production batches directly.

🏭

Production Batches

Plan, execute, and track manufacturing runs with status (planned → in progress → completed), quality checks, and expiry tracking.

📦

Finished Goods Inventory

Opening balance, current stock, quantities sold, manual adjustments, transaction history, and full valuation (FIFO/LIFO/Weighted Average).

⚠️

Low Stock Alerts

Push notification + email when stock drops to or below your set threshold (default: 5 units). Configurable per product.

📅

Expiry Date Tracking

Enable per-item expiry tracking on raw materials, simple products, composed products, and finished goods. Set a custom alert window (default: 30 days before expiry) per item. Items show visual expired/expiring-soon badges in the admin.

🔔

Expiry Alerts — Push & Email

A daily scheduled job (08:00 UTC) scans all tracked items across all 4 item types. When any item hits its alert window, the store owner gets a push notification + email digest listing item name, type, expiry date, and exact days remaining. Each item is only notified once per day.

🔄

Stock Adjustments

🏢

📝

Push Notifications — 3 Triggers

Firebase Cloud Messaging delivers real-time push to phone and browser for:

  • 📦 New Order — instant alert the moment a customer places an order
  • 📅 Expiring Stock — daily digest of items nearing or past their expiry date (raw materials, products, finished goods)
  • ⚠️ Low Stock — alert when any product stock falls to or below its threshold (default: 5 units)
